Privacy Policy

Effective Date: February 13, 2026

Aura Works ("we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how Aura Smart Silence handles your information.

1. Information Collection and Use

Aura Sleep Guard is designed with a "Privacy First" approach.

  • No Personal Data Collection: The app does not collect, store, or transmit any personal identification information (PII) such as your name, email address, or phone number to any external servers.
  • Local Processing: All automation logic, including sleep history and trigger detection (Charger, WiFi, Bluetooth), is processed entirely on your device. No data regarding your device's connection status or charging state is ever uploaded to our servers or shared with third parties.
  • Data Safety & Play Store Compliance: Our use of device state triggers is strictly for "App Functionality" purposes. We do not use these triggers for tracking or profiling. This approach ensures maximum privacy while providing intelligent automation.
  • DND Permission: The app requires "Do Not Disturb Access" purely to automate your notification settings according to your scheduled preferences. This permission is used exclusively for this purpose.

2. Third-Party Services

We use Google Play Services for internal app functionalities:

  • Google Play Billing: To process premium subscriptions. Payment details are handled securely by Google.
  • Google AdMob: To display advertisements in the free version of the app. AdMob may collect pseudonymous identifiers for ad personalization.

3. Data Retention

Application settings and sleep history are stored locally in your device's private storage. You can clear your sleep history at any time through the "History" section of the app. If you uninstall the app, all locally stored data will be deleted.

4. Children’s Privacy

Our application is safe for all users and does not target or knowingly collect data from children under the age of 13.

5. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.
